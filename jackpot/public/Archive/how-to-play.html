How to play Lotto Go ?
In order to purchase a ticket, you’ll need to have an Ethereum wallet. For help creating one, visit myetherwallet.com. On the homepage, click on the lottery you wish to play. You will be taken to a ticket page where you can choose your own set of numbers or click on the magic wand icon to have the numbers randomly chosen for you. Once you’ve begun selecting numbers, the magic wand icon will be replaced by a trash can icon. Clicking on the trash can icon will remove your selected numbers and the magic wand will once again appear as an option.
